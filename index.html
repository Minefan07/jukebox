Welcome to the Jukebox, the new and improved Minecraft Music Player. Complete with bugfixes and new features, it's obviously better than before. To play a specific song, type in its name in lowercase or else it won't work. To play multiple in order, separate multiple song names with commas. Make sure to spell the song correctly, with the exception of Oxygène and Équinoxe, in which case you can type it with or without the accent marks.
